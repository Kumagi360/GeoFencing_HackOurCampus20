Privacy





• We collect no data from your use of the MARTHA web tracker. Information may be collected by Google, OpenLayers, and OpenStreetMap by our use of their respective libraries and SDKs.
• All population data sent by the MARTHA app is anonymized through hashing. However, it is currently possible to track an individual because their hashed data is constant.
• This system is not GDPR compliant.
